Home Sweet Home
Title The Death of Dick Long
Director Daniel Scheinert
Lead Actor Michael Abbot Jr.
Rating R
Synopsis Something goes horrifically wrong during a rough and rowdy night of band practice, resulting in the brutal death of beloved friend Richard Long. Uncertain of what steps to take, the remaining members of Pink Freud, Zeke and Earl, do everything to avoid the inevitable questions that come with such a tragedy. A24 put it best when they wrote, "Daniel Scheinert 'expertly balances the hilarity and heartache of what happens when dark secrets are dragged kicking and screaming into the light.'"
